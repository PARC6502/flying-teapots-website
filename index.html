The Flying Teapots Circus is a society run by the dedicated students of the University of Sheffield. From beginners to experts The Flying Teapots offers a meeting place for people to learn, practice and teach a large variety of circus and performance skills, in a friendly, welcoming atmosphere. The tea and biscuits are free!

Some of the circus skills you'll find at teapots:

  • Juggling balls
  • Juggling clubs
  • Diabolo
  • Poi
  • Devil sticks
  • And many more!
